About Us

Founded in 1995, ElectroTech has grown to become a global leader in electronic manufacturing services. With state-of-the-art facilities and a team of highly skilled professionals, we deliver innovative solutions to clients across multiple industries.

Our commitment to quality, reliability, and continuous innovation has earned us recognition as a trusted partner for Fortune 500 companies and startups alike.

We operate with ISO 9001:2015 certification and adhere to the highest industry standards to ensure our products meet rigorous quality requirements.
